The Tube

A freeware minimalistic youtube browser based on youtube-dl 
and mplayer.

On start-up you can choose between 360p or 240p preference, and 
updating youtube-dl.

You can use the mouse or the keyboard for input. Press h or the
help button to get help on the keyboard shortcuts.

youtube-dl: rg3.github.io/youtube-dl/
mplayer: www.mplayerhq.hu/
